How to use this Calculator?

Step 1: Enter a First Lower Boundary of your Data.

Step 2: Enter First Upper Boundary of your Data.

Step 3: Enter how many rows of data you want.

Step 4: Click on Make Table, Enter the Frequency Values and Click on submit.

Step 5: Click on Calculte.

Don't Have a Data to fill?

Step 1: Click on Fill sample and then Click on Make table.

Step 2: Click on Fill sample to fill Frequency and Click on Submit.

Step 3: Now Click On Calculate and Feel the Magic.
